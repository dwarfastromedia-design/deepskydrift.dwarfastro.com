Make your astro image move

DeepSkyDrift turns a finished astrophotography image into a cinematic motion video. It reads the stars and structure already present in your image, builds a cleaner motion background, and animates selected stars into a smooth drift or fly-through effect.

No AI is used. No generative model invents stars, galaxies, or nebula detail. The motion is created from your image data in the browser.

Your image stays local on your device. Start with a JPG, PNG, or WebP, choose a preset, preview the motion, then export a short video for social, YouTube, or your own archive.
